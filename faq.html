Frequently Asked Questions

What prior experience do I need to join the bootcamp?

This program is designed for students who already have taken some coursework in computer programming / engineering and who are interested in pursuing it for a career. If you don't have this background but would still like to participate, contact us directly- we have other offerings which may fit your experience level better.

How intensive is the 4-week program? Can I work while attending?

This is an intensive, project-driven course requiring 30+ hours per week of commitment. Most successful students treat this as their primary focus during the 4 weeks.

While some students manage part-time work, we recommend clearing your schedule as much as possible to maximize your learning and project outcomes.

How is the course set up? Is it in-person, hybrid or remote?

This is ahybrid course with the option to be fully remote. You will also have access to our in-person office in Mountain View, CA, where you are free to drop by for co-working, mentorship, and extra support whenever you need it.

What makes this bootcamp different from online courses or tutorials?

Unlike passive learning, you'll build real enterprise-level projects that impress employers.

You also get:

• 1:1 mentorship from Silicon Valley engineers
• Personalized recommendation letters
• Algorithm training and LeetCode preparation
• Career support and referral opportunities

How does the 1:1 mentorship work?

You will have 1:1 access to senior Silicon Valley engineers focused on making you job-ready:

• Weekly 1-hour video sessions for guidance and code review
• Daily availability in-person and online for tutoring, support, and career strategy
• Exclusive insights into hiring and industry
• Resume review and portfolio optimization

Your mentor stays with you throughout the entire 4-week journey.

What kind of projects will I build during the bootcamp?

You'll create portfolio-worthy projects that demonstrate real-world skills:

• Full-stack web applications with modern industry frameworks
• AI-powered applications with state-of-the-art LLM technology
• Microservices architectures deployed on cloud platforms
• Open-source contributions to established projects

All projects are designed to help you gain enterprise-level experience, showcase your skills, and increase your hireability in the eyes of potential employers.

I have a scheduling conflict and can't make the track that I'm interested in.

Don't worry - we're flexible!

We understand that scheduling conflicts happen, so please reach out and we'll work with you to see what alternative arrangements are possible.

Contact us and we'll find a solution that works for you!

What happens after I complete the bootcamp?

Upon successful completion, you receive:

• Completed portfolio projects ready for job applications
• Personalized recommendation letter from your mentor
• Technical assessment report highlighting your strengths
• Referral opportunities to partner companies
• Alumni network access for continued support

Is there any guarantee I'll get an internship or job after the program?

While we cannot guarantee specific outcomes, our program is designed to maximize your competitiveness:

• 90%+ of graduates report that the program has made them a more competitive candidate
• Our recommendation letters have helped students secure positions at top-tier companies

Your success depends on your dedication and effort during the program.
