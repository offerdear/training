Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior programming experience to join the bootcamp?

No prior experience is required! Our program is designed to take you from zero to industry-ready. We start with fundamentals and build up to advanced concepts progressively.

However, having basic familiarity with computers and a strong willingness to learn will help you succeed faster.

How intensive is the 4-week program? Can I work while attending?

This is an intensive, project-driven bootcamp requiring 40+ hours per week of commitment. Most successful students treat this as their primary focus during the 4 weeks.

While some students manage part-time work, we recommend clearing your schedule as much as possible to maximize your learning and project outcomes.

What makes this bootcamp different from online courses or tutorials?

Unlike passive learning, you'll build real enterprise-level projects that impress employers and admissions committees.

You also get:

• 1:1 mentorship from Silicon Valley engineers
• Personalized recommendation letters
• Algorithm training and LeetCode preparation
• Career support and referral opportunities

Which track should I choose if I'm unsure about my career direction?

Consider your interests and career goals:

• Java/Spring Backend - Great for traditional software engineering roles
• Data Science/AI & LLM - Perfect for AI/ML careers and research
• DevOps & Microservices - Ideal for modern cloud architecture roles

We offer pre-program consultations to help you choose the best fit for your goals.

What kind of projects will I build during the bootcamp?

You'll create portfolio-worthy projects that demonstrate real-world skills:

• Full-stack web applications with modern frameworks
• AI-powered applications using cutting-edge LLM technology
• Microservices architectures deployed on cloud platforms
• Open-source contributions to established projects

All projects are designed to be impressive to HR teams and admissions officers.

How does the 1:1 mentorship work?

Each student is paired with a Silicon Valley engineer mentor who provides:

• Weekly 1-hour video sessions for guidance and code review
• Daily availability via Slack for quick questions
• Career advice and industry insights
• Personalized recommendation letter upon completion

Your mentor stays with you throughout the entire 4-week journey.

What happens after I complete the bootcamp?

Upon successful completion, you receive:

• Completed portfolio projects ready for job applications
• Personalized recommendation letter from your mentor
• Technical assessment report highlighting your strengths
• Referral opportunities to partner companies
• Alumni network access for continued support

Is there any guarantee I'll get an internship or job after the program?

While we cannot guarantee specific outcomes, our program is designed to maximize your competitiveness:

• 90%+ of graduates report feeling significantly more confident in technical interviews
• Students typically see 3-5x more interview callbacks after completing projects
• Our recommendation letters have helped students secure positions at top-tier companies

Your success depends on your dedication and effort during the program.

What are the technical requirements to participate?

You'll need:

• A laptop/computer capable of running development tools
• Stable internet connection for video calls and collaboration
• Willingness to install necessary software (we'll guide you through setup)
• GitHub account for project collaboration

We provide detailed setup instructions and technical support throughout the program.
